Vocals

Jerome Harris

Jerome has been singing all his life, and thanks his grandmother for first taking notice of his amazing singing ability.   In the early 70s, he sang gospel in San Francisco, started an a cappella group in high school, and formed the “2+2 Revue” while in the US Navy.  Jerome has performed with Bigcat Productions, in Richmond, California, and “This Music Matters Ministries”. In 2013, Jerome had the experience of a lifetime singing the national anthem at an Oakland A's home game. Jerome is the driving force and voice behind The Smokin' Slice of Mojo, and wishes for all to be filled with happiness as we bring back some of the joyful moments of your lifetime, playing timeless cover songs from the 60s, 70s, and 80s.
